ShareStreet is a crowdinvesting platform that lets investors discover, fund, and track real estate investments across Canada.

About


ShareStreet is an investment platform that connects investors with opportunities to fund real estate projects. We were founded when we learned there was a select group of investors who were reaping the benefits of highly attractive real estate investments.

We decided that by building an online platform for real estate under the crowdfunding exemption, we would tap into a large online investor base and give everyone an opportunity to invest in such attractive real estate investments.

We believe that this will be a valuable opportunity for existing and emerging real estate investors, as well as those looking to raise capital for their real estate projects. Due to current regulations, however, ShareStreet is only targeted to accredited and institutional investors. We look forward to being a major player in real estate crowdfunding once the exemption has been approved by the appropriate securities regulators.

Our Mission


In the long run, our aim is to provide everyone with an equal chance to invest in real estate while streamlining the process of online investing.
