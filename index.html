ShareStreet is Crowdinvesting Made Simple.

ShareStreet is a crowdinvesting platform that lets investors discover, fund, and track real estate investments across Canada.


At launch, ShareStreet will only cater to accredited investors, but our aim is to make investing in real estate accessible for everyone.

How it Works


  • Invest In A Project
  • Raise Capital

  • 1. Browse Investment Opportunities

    Join ShareStreet's online community of real estate investors, and browse our platform for available investment projects.

  • 2. Invest in a Project

    Find an investment that interests you based on the project profile, management team and required funding. Then, just follow our simple investment process to have your funds capitalized in no time.

  • 3. Track Your Investment's Growth

    View, track, and manage your investments in your ShareStreet account. Any questions? Simply contact one of our advisors.

  1. Submit Your Proposal

    If you want to raise funds for your own real estate project, follow our simple application process to submit your proposal to our team.

  2. Wait for Approval

    Once submitted, your application will undergo a thorough review process to verify your proposal.

  3. Monitor Your Funding Goals

    Upon acceptance, as an issuer, you'll be able to monitor investor progress as you get closer to your funding goal.
