How it works


  • Raise Capital
  • Invest

  1. Submit Your Application

    If you have a real estate project that you would like to raise funds for, simply follow our application process to submit your proposal to our team.

  2. Wait for Approval

    Once your application has been properly submitted, our team will do our due diligence to thoroughly review and verify your proposal.

  3. Monitor Funding Goals

    Upon acceptance, as a borrower, you will be able to stay up-to-date on your funding goal and monitor investor progress.

  1. Browse Investments

    Once you've joined our online community of real estate investors, you will be able to browse the available projects on our platform. You will have access to view the information on the project, the management team and the required funding.

  2. Invest

    If you find a project that interests you, you can simply follow our investment process and have your funds capitalized in no time.

  3. Manage your investments

    Continue to view and manage your investments online through your ShareStreet account. For any questions, comments or concerns, visit our contact page to see how you can get in touch with a service representative.
